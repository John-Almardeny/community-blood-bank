What is CBB?

"Community Blood Bank (CBB) is a non-profit blood bank based on the community to provide a blood supply for all patients who are in need in Waterford County. CBB is affiliated to the Health Service Executive in Ireland and it constitutes the largest group of blood providers and collectors. CBB covers a wide area of Waterford Hospitals and Medical Centres; generous volunteers come from all over Waterford to donate their bloods."

New Zika Virus-Related Guidelines for Blood Donation in the United States
As the Zika virus is spreading widely in the United States and Latin America, the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has issued a set of guidelines for accepting blood donations in these regions. The FDA has stated that blood banks should not obtain blood from people who have recently traveled to the Zika-epidemic countries. The virus is being actively transmitted through mosquitoes for the past four weeks in these areas.
